

## Update M-Pesa Credentials

You want to re-enter your M-Pesa credentials with the correct values. I'll prompt you to update all 5 secrets:

1. **MPESA_CONSUMER_KEY** - From your Daraja app
2. **MPESA_CONSUMER_SECRET** - From your Daraja app
3. **MPESA_PASSKEY** - The Lipa Na M-Pesa Online passkey (this is likely the one causing ResultCode 17)
4. **MPESA_SHORTCODE** - Your Paybill/Till number
5. **MPESA_TILL_NUMBER** - Your Till number

### Where to find these:
- **Consumer Key & Secret**: Safaricom Daraja Portal → Your App → Keys
- **Passkey**: Daraja Portal → APIs → Lipa Na M-Pesa Online → Your app's passkey
- **Shortcode/Till**: Your M-Pesa business registration documents

After updating, I'll test with a small donation to verify everything works.
